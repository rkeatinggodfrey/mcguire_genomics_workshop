Module 1: Introduction to Genomics
==================================

This session introduces students to the vocabulary needed to understand genomics, genome assembly, annotation, and bioinformatics. Students will learn the vocabulary, as well as determine how to communicate this information to lay audiences effectively. The class will be structured as a lecture introducing essential terms and their uses in the context of genomics and bioinformatics, followed by a student-centered activity. This will allow students to work in teams to recognize, define, and explain the new vocabulary introduced in the lecture. After completing the activity, student groups will discuss their answers and work to confirm or correct other groups’ responses. By the end of the session, students will be able to understand and apply genomics and bioinformatics vocabulary in future sessions.
